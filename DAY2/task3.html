Explore Paris


Top Attractions

  1. the majestic Eiffel Tower

Head to Trocadero Square for unbeatable Eiffel Tower photo ops! The steps and railings provide countless ways to compose your perfect shot. Visit Trocadero, one of the best photo spots in Paris but watch out – it’s usually crowded with busloads of tourists. For unbeatable pictures, arrive at Trocadero during sunrise; winter sunsets behind the tower are unbeatable!

  • The majestic Notre Dame de Paris

    • Allow me to draw your attention to the spectacular Notre Dame Cathedral. An iconic backdrop for any photograph, Notre Dame dazzles with its stunning beauty when caught in the right light – particularly during the sweeping splendor of a Parisian sunset.

  • France’s historic ‘Quartier Latin’

    • Stroll through Quartier Latin’s inviting cafes and cobbled streets on the left bank of the Seine in the 5th and 6th arrondissements – there’s so much to discover! Don’t miss my favorite hideout: Odette Patisserie. The cream puffs are truly out of this world and will make your mouth water with anticipation. So come, and experience Quartier Latin’s magical charms today!

  • La Maison Rose

    • Picture perfection awaits down Rue de l’Abreuvoir! La Maison Rose’s candy pink and green façade has skyrocketed in Insta fame, beckoning shutterbugs. To get the shot sans the crowds (and cars!), get there early – before it opens. For a different vibe, show up when La Maison Rose is bustling and its terrace alive with chatter. Whichever you choose, this is Paris at her picture-perfectly photogenic best.

  • arch of Pont Alexandre

    • The Pont Alexandre III is an extravagant feast for the eyes and a photographer’s paradise! This majestic bridge commands incredible views of the Seine, with breathtaking backdrops of the Eiffel Tower. Bookmark this spot for your next Paris sunset adventure – admire it from the bridge, or capture those special moments from Quai de Seine further off to the right.
